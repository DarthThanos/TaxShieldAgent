## Description (500 words max)

If you sell online, every state you ship to is quietly counting your sales. Cross a threshold — and most states set it at $100,000 — and you suddenly owe sales tax. Miss it, and the first you hear about it is a letter from the Department of Revenue demanding back taxes, penalties, and interest.

That is the problem TaxShieldAgent solves.

We monitor your sales across Stripe, Shopify, Etsy, PayPal, Square, and Amazon in real time. When your revenue in any state approaches its Economic Nexus threshold, we alert you inside your Stripe Dashboard — right where you already work. No new tabs, no separate login, no weekly spreadsheets.

Each state in the US has its own rules for when an out-of-state seller owes sales tax. Some count transactions, some count revenue, some count both. The thresholds range from $100,000 down to $10,000. Keeping track of all 50 states across 6 platforms is a full-time job. TaxShieldAgent does it automatically.

**How it works:**

1. Install TaxShieldAgent from the Stripe App Marketplace. Connect your other platforms (Shopify, Etsy, etc.) from our dashboard — it takes 2 minutes.
2. We aggregate your sales data by state across all your connected platforms. Our nexus engine knows every state's current thresholds and rules.
3. When you approach a threshold, you see a yellow, red, or critical alert right in your Stripe Dashboard. Each alert tells you exactly how close you are: "$87,400 of $100,000 in California (87%)."
4. When you are ready to register, tap the "FIX — $1" button. We handle the state tax registration through Stripe Tax for a flat $1 per state. No percentage fees, no monthly minimums.

## Privacy Policy Summary
TaxShieldAgent accesses your Stripe payment data (transaction amounts and customer states) solely to calculate Economic Nexus exposure. We aggregate sales totals by state — we never store individual customer names, addresses, or payment details. Connected platform data (Shopify, Etsy, etc.) is processed the same way: aggregated by state, then the raw data is discarded. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. You can disconnect any platform and delete your data at any time from the TaxShieldAgent dashboard. Full privacy policy: https://taxshieldagent.com/privacy
